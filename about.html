Our Story

For years, Dondlinger General Contracting has been a cornerstone of the Wisconsin Rapids community. We don't just build structures; we build relationships. Our "Design-Build" philosophy ensures that your vision never gets lost in translation between architects and builders.

We specialize in full-service residential and commercial contracting. From the moment we sit down for a "napkin sketch" consultation to the final walkthrough, our team is on-site, ensuring every detail meets our rigorous standards.

Based in Wisconsin Rapids, WI, we understand the unique demands of local climate and code, making us the trusted choice for hundreds of homeowners across Central Wisconsin.
