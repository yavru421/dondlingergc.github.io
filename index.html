How to install

The install prompt appears automatically when you open the calculator.

  1. iPhone (Safari): Open dondlingergc.com/calc/ in Safari → tap the Share ⎡ button → tap Add to Home Screen.
  2. Android (Chrome): Open the calculator and tap the Install App button that appears at the top of the screen.
  3. Launch it from your home screen — it works fully offline from there.
